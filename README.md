# Light Caster: Abyssal Survivor ⚔️✨

## 📖 Description
**Light Caster: Abyssal Survivor** is an endless, wave-based 2D survival shooter built in Python using Pygame. Plunged into the Abyssal Depths, you must harness the power of Light and Resolve to fend off relentless geometric entities. Manage your endurance, dodge dynamic hazards, unlock powerful abilities like "Supreme Judgement," and survive long enough to face the Abyssal Bosses!

## ✨ Features
* **Dynamic Level Generation:** Progress through 30+ levels with escalating difficulty, shifting wall layouts, and randomized spawn behaviors.
* **Multi-Phase Combat:** Survive the "Normal" countdown, endure the frantic "Madness" phase, and conquer brutal Boss encounters.
* **Resource Management:** Carefully balance your Light (ammo), Resolve (HP), and Endurance (sprint energy). 
* **7 Unique Abyssal Entities:** Face off against enemies with distinct behaviors, including line-of-sight shooters, light-draining thieves, and shadow-casting demons.
* **Supreme Judgement:** Charge up a devastating holy blast to wipe out nearby enemies in a pinch.
* **Persistent Progression:** Game progress automatically saves locally to your machine.

## 🎮 Controls
* **Aim & Move:** Mouse Cursor
* **Cast Light (Shoot):** Left Mouse Button `OR` Spacebar
* **Sprint:** Hold Right Mouse Button
* **Reload Light:** Press `R` (Takes 2.5s, Max 5 uses per life)
* **Supreme Judgement:** Press `A` (Charges a delayed, massive area-of-effect blast)
* **Revive:** Press `R` when dead to consume a revive (Max 2 per run)

## 👾 Enemy Guide
* 🔴 **Red Doubt (Circle):** Basic chaser entity.
* 🟢 **Green Deceiver (Diamond):** Fires projectiles if it gains a clear line-of-sight.
* 🟣 **Pink Thief (Hexagon):** Drains your Light (Ammo) upon contact.
* 🟠 **Orange Blessing (Cross):** Friendly entity! Catch it for a shield buff and Resolve recovery.
* 🌑 **Dark Shadow (Star - Lvl 9+):** Spawns massive dark blind-zones periodically.
* 🟪 **Curse (Star - Lvl 12+):** Causes massive Resolve and Light damage on touch.
* 👻 **Phantom (Triangle - Lvl 15+):** Alternates between vulnerable and invincible ghost states.
* 🛑 **Abyssal Bosses (Octagon):** Features bullet hell fireballs, map hazards, and invincible shield phases.
